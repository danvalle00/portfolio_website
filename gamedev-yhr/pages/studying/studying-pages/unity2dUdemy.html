Complete C# Unity 2D Game Development

I'm studying the Unity Engine and C# which is area I'm really want to be more proficient at. The course I'm doing is the "Complete C# Unity 2D Game Development" on Udemy by GameDev.tv.

Section 1 - Intro & Setup

There isn't much to learn here, its just the setup of the Unity and its Editor

Section 2 - Delivery Dash project

In this section I was introduced to the basics of Unity and C#, by making a 2D delivery car game much of this I already know but I decided to go through it anyway to refresh my memory.

Section 2 Notes

A lot of things in this lessons was out-of-date but I choose to follow along updating the things, like the Input System And I feel it was a little weird the way sprites was handled, like the proportions and pixels per unit.
