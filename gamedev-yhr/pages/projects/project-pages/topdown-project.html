About the Project

loner-lander is my recreation of the classic Lunar Lander game. In this game I made a twist of having different planets and different gravities and also increasing difficulty because the platforms get smaller as you progress.

Key Features

  • Basic UI with TextMeshPro
  • MultiScene and High Score System
  • Multi Camera Angles
